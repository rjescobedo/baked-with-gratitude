My Sourdough Classes

About the Classes

Our hands-on sourdough baking classes are designed for all skill levels. Learn how to create and care for a starter, knead and shape dough, and bake delicious loaves that will impress your friends and family.

What You'll Learn:

  • How to make and maintain a sourdough starter
  • The science behind fermentation and dough proofing
  • Shaping and scoring techniques
  • Baking methods for crusty and flavorful bread

Join us and discover the joy of sourdough baking!
