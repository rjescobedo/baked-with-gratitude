About The Baker

Hello, my name is Enid Paradowski and I'm a wife, proud mom to two energetic boys, and a passionate home baker with a love for sourdough bread. My baking journey began as a simple hobby during quiet afternoons and quickly grew into a meaningful tradition that I cherish. There’s something magical about transforming just a few ingredients into a warm, golden loaf that fills my home with the comforting aroma of fresh bread.

Baking sourdough is more than a craft for me—it’s a way to slow down, connect with my family, and savor the simple joys of life. I love experimenting with flavors, trying new techniques, and sharing my creations with friends and loved ones. Each loaf tells its own story, and every slice is made with care and love.

When I’m not in the kitchen, I’m a full-time mom, adventuring with my two boys who keep me on my toes. Whether it’s soccer games, art projects, or exploring the outdoors, my family inspires me to be creative and live life to the fullest. My husband is my greatest supporter and my taste-tester in chief—he never hesitates to offer a smile or an honest opinion on my latest bakes.

I also have a passion for creating warm, inviting spaces where people can come together over good food and conversation. Whether it’s a simple family dinner or a gathering with friends, I believe that sharing a meal builds connection and joy.

Thank you for stopping by my little corner of the world. I hope my journey inspires you to explore your passions, create something meaningful, and find beauty in everyday moments. Let’s share some warmth and flavor, one loaf at a time!
